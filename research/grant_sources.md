# Free Grant Search Sources & Databases

*Curated for Delta Rising Foundation — February 2026*

These are the sources our scraper/monitoring system should target. All have free access.

---

## Tier 1 — Primary Sources (scrape first)

### 1. Grants.gov
- **URL:** https://www.grants.gov
- **Type:** Federal government grants
- **Access:** Free, has XML API (no key needed for basic search)
- **Content:** All federal grant opportunities
- **Scrapability:** Has API + structured data — easiest to automate
- **Relevant categories:** Environment, Community Development, Natural Resources

### 2. Grantseeker.io
- **URL:** https://search.grantseeker.io/
- **Type:** Comprehensive nonprofit/foundation database
- **Access:** Free
- **Content:** Foundation and nonprofit grant data
- **Scrapability:** Web-based, needs Selenium for dynamic content

### 3. FindGrant.ai
- **URL:** https://findgrant.ai/
- **Type:** AI-powered grant discovery
- **Access:** Free
- **Content:** Uses IRS Form 990 analysis + natural language search
- **Scrapability:** API-like natural language interface

### 4. Philanthropy News Digest (PND)
- **URL:** https://philanthropynewsdigest.org/
- **Type:** Grant announcements / news
- **Access:** Free (part of Candid network)
- **Content:** Curated RFPs and grant announcements
- **Scrapability:** Has RSS feeds — very easy to monitor

---

## Tier 2 — Secondary Sources

### 5. Grantmakers.io
- **URL:** https://www.grantmakers.io/
- **Type:** Foundation data from IRS 990s
- **Access:** Free, open-source
- **Content:** Who funds what, grant amounts, grantee lists
- **Use case:** Find foundations that fund orgs similar to Delta

### 6. Candid (formerly Foundation Center + GuideStar)
- **URL:** https://candid.org/
- **Type:** Comprehensive funder profiles
- **Access:** Free tier (limited), full access at public libraries
- **Tip:** Many public libraries provide free Candid/Foundation Directory access

### 7. EPA Grants
- **URL:** https://www.epa.gov/grants
- **Type:** Environmental Protection Agency funding
- **Access:** Free
- **Content:** Environmental justice, climate, pollution grants
- **Relevant to:** All four Delta programs

### 8. California Grants Portal
- **URL:** https://www.grants.ca.gov/
- **Type:** California state grants
- **Access:** Free
- **Content:** State-level grant opportunities
- **Relevant to:** Delta is HQ'd in Garden Grove, CA

## Tier 3 — Niche / Thematic Sources

### 9. National Science Foundation (NSF)
- **URL:** https://www.nsf.gov/funding/
- **Relevant to:** AI Climate Tools program
- **Focus:** Research grants for science-based solutions

### 10. USDA — Community Facilities Grants
- **URL:** https://www.rd.usda.gov/
- **Relevant to:** Resilience Nursery, community projects
- **Focus:** Rural community development

### 11. Arbor Day Foundation
- **URL:** https://www.arborday.org/
- **Relevant to:** Resilience Nursery, More Shade
- **Focus:** Tree planting, community forests

### 12. Climate Investment Funds
- **URL:** https://www.cif.org/
- **Relevant to:** CBECN program
- **Focus:** Indigenous peoples, carbon markets
