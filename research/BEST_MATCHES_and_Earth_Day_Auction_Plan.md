## EARTH DAY SUSTAINABILITY SHOWCASE — AUCTION PLAN (April 25, 2026)

The team is right — an auction on Earth Day makes perfect sense. It lets Delta accept in-kind donations of ALL kinds (tickets, food, spa services, hotel stays, seeds, dirt, shirts, whatever) and turn them into cash.

### How a Silent Auction Works

1. **Collect donated items** from companies (in-kind donations)
2. **Display items on tables** with bid sheets (or use a free mobile bidding app)
3. **Guests write bids** — starting bid = 30-50% of retail value, minimum bid increments ($5-$10)
4. **Bidding stays open** during the event (2-3 hours typically)
5. **Highest bidder wins** when time closes — pay and take their item
6. **Delta keeps 100% of the proceeds**

### Free Auction Tools
- **32Auctions** (32auctions.com) — Free plan for up to 100 items
- **BiddingOwl** (biddingowl.com) — Free for nonprofits, mobile bidding
- **Handbid** — Free trial available

### Tips for Maximizing Revenue

- **Start bids at 30-50% of retail value** — drives bidding competition
- **Group small items into baskets** — "Garden Lover's Package" (seeds + tools + gift card) performs better than individual seed packets
- **Have 1-2 premium items** — a Ritz-Carlton stay or Disneyland tickets as the anchor that draws people in
- **Use a "Buy It Now" option** — for some items, set a high Buy It Now price for instant purchase (150-200% of value)
- **Promote top items beforehand** — post pictures on social media a week before to build excitement
